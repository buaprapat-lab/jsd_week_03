Our Story

The Founders' Night Out

It all started with an open kitchen and an unpredictable night. Barkbowl wasn't born in a boardroom, but at this very table. We believe great ideas can come from unexpected places — like this legendary evening when a group of old friends (including a spotted leader, a watchful senior, a joyful black guardian, and a very fluffy one) decided to settle in for a serious discussion over some bottles of white wine.

As the wine poured, the dream became clear: to create a cozy highland retreat thats just as fun and welcoming for dogs and their people as this unforgettable dinner was. Every handmade treat and cozy nook at Barkbowl is inspired by the whimsical spirit of that magical night.
